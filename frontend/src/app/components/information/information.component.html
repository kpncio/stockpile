Cookies & Sessions

Cloudflare Services

Trader does not read, write, or interact with any cookies, local sessions, or local storage; however, KPNC Technology uses Cloudflare services to provide users with the best experience and to protect our own infrastructure. Cloudflare sits in between your client and our origin servers and may read, write, or interact with their own cookies, local sessions, or local storage for a variety of reasons.

For more information see Cloudflare's Privacy Policy...

Market Data

Tiingo API, IEX Cloud, Yahoo Finance

Historical and intraday data on over 500 of the top stocks, currencies, and crypto currencies is provided by Tiingo API with supplimental data being being provided by IEX Cloud. Historical and intraday data on metal and energy futures are provided by Yahoo Finance. The underlying stock, forex, and crypto information being displayed in Trader is provided by Tiingo and IEX. Information on precious metals and other commodities are from Metals Live.

If you are interested in market data see Tiingo API, IEX Cloud and Yahoo Finance for more information...

Backend Technologies

Cloudflare Workers/KV, Beautiful Soup, CloudScraper, YFinance

Cloudflare Workers and Workers KV allow for low latency requests from users around the world. Python data scraping packages including Beautiful Soup, Cloudscraper, and YFinance make collecting publicly available data a breeze.

See more information at Workers, Beautiful Soup, CloudScraper, and YFinance...

Frontend Technologies

Angular, ECharts, Lightweight Charts

The frontend of Trader uses Google's Angular framework for a powerful and performant experience. However, data visualization is arguably just as important in trading so Trader uses Apache's ECharts for general charting and TradingView's Lightweight Charts for financial specific charting. Lightweight Charts provides fast and easy financial based charts that are incredibly customizable.

See more information at Angular, ECharts, and Lightweight Charts...
